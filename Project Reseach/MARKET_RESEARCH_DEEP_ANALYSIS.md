### Paper 7: "The Probability of Backtest Overfitting" - Pardo (2012)

**Why This Matters**: Mathematically quantify how likely your backtest is fake.

**The Problem**:
```

â”œâ”€ Results: 25% annual return

â”œâ”€ accuracy_metric = how good is backtest

â”‚  â”œâ”€ Sharpe ratio 1-2: Moderate overfitting risk

â”œâ”€ More optimization (more trials) = more overfitting risk
â”œâ”€ Higher Sharpe alone = not enough (could be overfitted to high Sharpe)
â”œâ”€ Walk-forward testing = dramatically reduces POO
â””â”€ Proper validation = only way to trust result
```
